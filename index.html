Reflection Leader Ship Creed #8

"I wil strive to know myself better and seek self-improvement." Learning about this line in the creed is of great value to me. One because I believe everyone should apply this to their life. Two, there is always room for improvement. To start it might be a bit difficult, having to realize your strengths and weaknesses. Once you do that, it'd be more helpful to write them down and read them over, look into your weaknesses and see how you can improve upon it. When making those improvements, you shouldn't just rush into, that usually causes you to be more flustered and more prone to doing the opposite, instead take it one step at a time. Once I get more used to a certain thing, I continue and make challenges for myself, such as making my own hack-nights and seeing what I can get done in a certain amount of time. I look back each time, see what happened that I didn't execute correctly and iterate the next time I do it. The most important message it has to me is you should always try to know yourself as a person, once you do that, look into how you can change and be better, and when you think you're at your peak, make the list all over and repeat the process. Keep Improving. Don't Settle.

Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla was an inventor, with that in mind, he shaped the future, the present for us. He invented A/C, The tesla coil,The Magnifying Transmitter, The Tesla Turbine, Shadowgraphs/X-RAYS, Radio, The Induction Motor, and multiple other inventions. Yet despite all of this, he ended up being broke, perhaps it's not the type of life most of us are looking to seek, he didn't care, he cared more about the thought of his inventions. And if they were used to invest in a better future, he was all for it. As a young boy, his mother was an inventive homemaker who created household appliances. He had an eidetic memory, received early education from Smiljan. He attended the Austrian Polytechnic in Graz, Austria; he was doing very well in his first year, however, he got addicted to gambling in his second, causing him to ruin his graduation and unable to obtain a degree. The point being, he had a great start but he ruined it, that didn't stop him from becoming one of the most influental persons who once roamed this Earth.
